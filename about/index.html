About the Site

Full Speed runs on Movable Type. Punctuation is educated by SmartyPants. The background pattern was created by Alan Hettinger and was found in the excellent gallery of pixel patterns at Kaliber1000. Full Speed is hosted at TextDrive.

Syndication

In the past, we have offered a variety of syndicated feed formats to ensure compatibility with all newsreaders. In an approach to further our goal of universal compatibility, we now use one feed, hosted by FeedBurner. This feed is automatically served in a format that your browser or news aggregator will understand.

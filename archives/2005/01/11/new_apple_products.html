[ January 11, 2005 ]

New Apple Products

Apple unveiled many new products at the MacWorld SF Expo today:

  • Mac Mini — an entry-level G4 starting at $499
  • iPod shuffle — no display, 512MB $99, 1GB $149
  • iWork — Keynote 2 + Pages, the new word processor, $79 or free with new Mac
  • iLife ‘05
  • Final Cut Express HD

Sadly, there were no PowerBook updates.

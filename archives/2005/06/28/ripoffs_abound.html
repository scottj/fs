[ June 28, 2005 ]

Ripoffs Abound

Exhibit A:

  1. Contact Sheet
  2. Pitchfork
  3. Kottke
Now, at least Kottke gave credit to Pitchfork, but I find it to be quite the coincidence that each of the images that these articles are centered around is 200 pixels tall. It seems to me that these articles about Nike ripping off Dischord have themselves ripped off some imagery. Please note that I’m not condoning Nike’s acts; I actually agree with these articles. I just really doubt that they have asked for permission to use these images.

Update: Nike has apologized. I’m impressed.
